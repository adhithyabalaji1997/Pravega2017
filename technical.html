TECHNICAL EVENTS

These events aim to cater to a much wider groups of people that might directly exclude scientific research

ENGINEERING

Laser Tag Infinity

Play a live version of Counter Strike (except with laser guns) - in various game modes, including deathmatch, bomb and capture the flag.

Laser Maze

Navigate a series of laser tripwires, Mission Impossble style to win a prize. Beware: not for faint heart! (not as easy as it sounds)

Bat-Man

A challenge of ultrasonic levels. Use an ultrasonic device to learn how to navigate a maze while blindfolded - learn some interesting science while you're at it!

GAMING

FIFA

Everyone's favourite football simulation. Players fight one on one multiplayer matches in knockout format.

Counter Strike (CS-GO)

This event is organized for the professionals. Clash with teams from all over India in the fight for Counter Strike supremacy, with prizes worth INR 25000 on the line.

DOTA

Get your heroes and supports geared up for the fight. DOTA at IISc aims to bring together the gaming community in its first ever run of Defence of the Ancients.

WORKSHOPS

Meet with a like minded group of people, engage in intensive discussion and explore the unfamiliar with Pravega’s multitude of workshops. Topics include ViR Game Development, IoT, Big Data and many more.

Stay tuned with our social media outlets to keep updated with the list of workshops. Register before all seats are taken!

LITERARY DECATHLON

The Literary Decathlon is an amalgamation of various literary posers, that will test the literary and vocabulary skills, as well as the general awareness of the participants.

This pioneering event’s name is derived from its ten rounds, including a special qualifier round, which is called the Mixed Bag. Progressive rounds test the speaking skills of the participants as well as their ability to display quick wit to a given situation.
